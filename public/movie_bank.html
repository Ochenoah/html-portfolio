MOVIE MEMORY BANK

A COLLECTION OF COMMENTARIES ABOUT MOVIES I'VE WATCHED

A BEAUTIFUL MIND

I remember feeling so connected to this dramatized story about John Nash. I had feelings to do great things and be considered a beautiful mind as well. I was in school when I first watched the movie. I can't remember the date as of now but it was in my university days.

THE NOTEBOOK

So far, I've actually watched this movie just once. I remember that I use to see it as a recommendation everytime I went online for some movie recommendation. I felt like there was some kind of hype about it being one of the great romantic movies and that was a turn off for me. Eventually I watched it and was actually pleasantly surprised. I didn't think I'd like it but I ended up liking it.

THE ULTIMATE GIFT

I've actually watched this one several times. I used to cry when watching it but I haven't watched so there is no telling how I'd feel watching it again. I liked that it talked about things as gifts and showed the maturing process of the main character from a vain and entitled child to a living being conscious of of others and acknowledging the importance of Love in Life.
